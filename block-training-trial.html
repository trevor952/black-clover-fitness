What Is Block Training

Not a class.
Not a workout.
A coaching system.

Block Training at BCF is a 45-minute, coach-led session built around two things that actually move the needle: functional strength training and built-in metabolic conditioning.

We don't track calories burned. We don't celebrate how sweaty you got. We care about the things that actually change your body and your life — getting stronger, moving better, building the kind of conditioning that carries over into everything you do.

Every session is programmed and coached. You're not wandering around a gym floor guessing what to do next. You show up, you get coached, you get results.

01
Functional Strength First
Every session is anchored in compound, functional movements that build real-world strength — not just mirror muscles. Progressive overload is built into the program so you keep getting stronger week over week.
02
Built-In Metabolic Conditioning
Metabolic work is structured into every session — not slapped on at the end as an afterthought. It complements the strength work, not competes with it. Your conditioning improves as your strength does.
03
Capped at 10. Always Coached.
Every Block session has a maximum of 10 people and a coach running it. You get attention, correction, and accountability — not just a playlist and a timer.
